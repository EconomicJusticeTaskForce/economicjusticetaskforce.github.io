HOW WE HAVE HELPED

Through a range of services such as counseling, legal assistance, and emergency shelter, organizations in Massachusetts provide vital support to survivors of domestic and sexual violence. These services offer survivors a safe space to process trauma, access resources, and rebuild their lives. Additionally, community education, engagement, and prevention initiatives help raise awareness and prevent future instances of violence. By empowering survivors with the tools and support they need, Massachusetts organizations play a crucial role in helping individuals break free from abusive situations and move towards healing and empowerment.
